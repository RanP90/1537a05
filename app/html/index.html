Panda is a shy boy who will hide at first until he feels safe to come out. Once you have gained his trust he will head butt you when you come home from work to tell you he loves you and missed you. He loves to look out the window from his cat tree, and likes to seek out high places (like closets) to hide.
Dennis and Missy are a bonded pair who are both cuddle bugs that love lots of pets and attention. Missy will come to you as soon as you see her. Dennis is a little more shy and will wait for you to initiate the pets, but once you do he doesn’t want you to stop!
Boots was a spicy feral kitty when he first arrived at the rescue. Over time with lots of love from his foster mom, and with his fellow foster brothers and sisters showing him how to cat, he has become an extremely affectionate boy. In fact, when the house is quiet, you will find it hard to ignore his attention. He truly loves to be with other cats and would need to go to a home with another kitty pal. He loves scratches around his head and belly, and is currently figuring out how to be a lap cat. He watches intently and you can see he wants to do it too. Boots is a handsome tomcat who’s a talker, but his voice is very soft. He is such a lover with so much love to give.
